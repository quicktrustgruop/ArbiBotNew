About ArbiBot

ArbiBot is a multi-network cryptocurrency arbitrage system designed to identify and execute profitable arbitrage opportunities across various decentralized exchanges (DEXs) and blockchains.

The bot utilizes flash loan capabilities to perform arbitrage operations without requiring substantial upfront capital. It continuously monitors price differentials between different DEXs and automatically executes trades when profitable opportunities are detected.

Key Features:
  • Multi-Network Support: Operates across Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Optimism.
  • Flash Loan Integration: Leverages flash loans from major protocols like Aave, dYdX, and Uniswap V3.
  • Intelligent Risk Management: Applies sophisticated risk assessment algorithms to evaluate opportunities.
  • Automated Execution: Automatically executes identified arbitrage opportunities.
  • Profit Distribution: Automatically distributes profits to configured wallets.
  • Cloud Mining Simulation: Simulates mining activity with a dashboard.
  • Real-time Monitoring: Provides real-time insights into bot performance, opportunities, and transactions.

How It Works
  1. Opportunity Detection:

    The bot continuously scans supported DEXs across multiple blockchains to identify price differences for the same token pairs.

  2. Risk Assessment:

    Detected opportunities undergo risk assessment, considering factors like:

    • Price difference magnitude
    • Gas costs
    • Market liquidity
    • Potential slippage

  3. Flash Loan Execution:

    For profitable opportunities, the bot:

    • Takes a flash loan from supported protocols
    • Buys tokens on the DEX with lower price
    • Sells tokens on the DEX with higher price
    • Repays the flash loan with interest
    • Collects the profit

  4. Profit Distribution:

    Profits are automatically distributed according to the wallet configuration.
